the value of the photography.

Photography's an art form. As photographer, I appreciate the artistic value that's in photography. Photography express and bring out the beauty in front of us.

the experience.

There's a lot more to photography than the push of a button. Amidst my pursuits of documenting the perfect moment, I take new challenges everyday. I face the uncertainties that come along with weather, lighting, and more.

the final product.

I love the process of putting together the perfect photos. There's much more to a photo session than the shoot itself. I have the joy in editing, using my creativity, and picking out the best images.
